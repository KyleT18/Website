Brief History

The 6'7" swingman played high school basketball for Little Rock Central High School and college basketball for the University of Arkansas.
After two years with Arkansas, he declared for the 2001 NBA draft where he was drafted 10th overall by the Boston Celtics.
The seven-time NBA All-Star has also played for the Phoenix Suns, Atlanta Hawks and Brooklyn Nets, while having also represented the United States national team.
