Brief History

Haslem played college basketball for the University of Florida, where he was a key member of four Florida Gators NCAA tournament teams.
He played professional basketball in France for a year, and then signed with the Miami Heat as an undrafted free agent in 2003.
He has won three NBA championships while playing for the Heat.
